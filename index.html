About Me

I started coding amateurishly in 2017 before completing a coding BootCamp at Case Western Reserve University later that year. I grew up in a rural part of Akron, Ohio known as Portage Lakes, but I currently reside in Cleveland's Tremont neighborhood where I enjoy the local restaurants and bars. I also have a garden there that keeps me busy. Other hobbies include brewing beer, following the Detroit Red Wings and cooking all kinds of cuisine.

My previous lines of work that led me to coding are varied including; forge worker, line cook, cobbler and Zamboni driver. After stumbling around for longer than I'd like to admit, I found my calling in programming and haven't looked back since.
